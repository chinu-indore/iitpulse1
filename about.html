What is IIT-PULSE ?

IIT-PULSE is a central India’s first institute of its kind where student get the complete teaching package, proper direction and quality education from the mentors. Since beginning the objective is to enable each aspirants to achieve success in JEE (MAIN & ADVANCED), NEET and AIIMS. The Founder of IIT-PULSE and renowned Physics guru Sunil Gupta Sir has built a strong reputation at national level for their excellence in teaching and promoting a broad range of courses that are up to date and relevant in today’s fast changing professional world .

IIT-PULSE have years of vast experience in coaching, thousands of students prepared for these highly competitive examination. All the faculties are highly qualified and well trained in the latest coaching techniques to make sure their students get the biggest advantage and satisfaction. The IIT-PULSE believes that effective guidance is the primary need of every student which would create motivation, courage and confidence to face all challenges.

JEE (MAIN & ADVANCED), NEET and AIIMS are toughest examinations to be taken by a student. Over the years IIT’s, NIT’S, AIIM’s have created world class educational platforms for Indian and foreign students. IIT-PULSE makes the environment easy and comfortable such a way where students feel comfortable and confident in competitive environment, and stimulate the student’s own strength to fight with best brains. Institute provide excellent course material with high standards based on extensive analysis of competitive exams over the years and the latest developments in all the subjects
